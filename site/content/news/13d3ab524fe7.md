Sierra, the customer service agent startup founded by
Bret Taylor
, announced on Thursday that it has acquired the
YC-backed French startup Fragment
, which helps businesses integrate AI into workflows.
This is Sierra’s third public acquisition.

which it acquired in late March
) and voice agent company Receptive AI (
which it also announced it acquired in late March
).

Fragment co-founders Olivier Moindrot and Guillaume Genthial will be joining the Sierra team.
